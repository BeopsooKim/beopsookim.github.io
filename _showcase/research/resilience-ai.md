<h3 class="h5">AI and Optimization for Grid Resilience</h3>
<p>
My work applies optimization, statistical learning, and AI-assisted decision support to power-grid
planning and operation under uncertainty, high renewable penetration, and disturbance risk.
</p>
<p class="small text-muted mb-2">
Core questions: how to turn data, forecasts, and physical constraints into decisions that improve
reliability, reduce cost, and preserve operational feasibility.
</p>
<ul class="small mb-2">
    <li>Deep-learning and hybrid-model comparisons for load forecasting in power systems.</li>
    <li>Particle swarm optimization for distributed generation allocation under fault-current constraints.</li>
    <li>Reliability-aware maintenance, replacement, and resilience planning for grid assets.</li>
</ul>
<p class="small mb-0">
Representative work:
<a href="https://doi.org/10.1155/2024/5587728" target="_blank">deep-learning load forecasting</a>,
<a href="https://doi.org/10.3390/en14020418" target="_blank">DG allocation with PSO</a>, and
<a href="https://doi.org/10.1007/s42835-021-00979-z" target="_blank">underground cable maintenance optimization</a>.
</p>
