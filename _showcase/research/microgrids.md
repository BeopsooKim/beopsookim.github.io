<h3 class="h5">Microgrids and Distributed Energy Resources</h3>
<p>
I study microgrids and distributed energy resources as operational platforms for resilient,
low-carbon power supply in isolated, campus-scale, and critical-load environments.
</p>
<p class="small text-muted mb-2">
Core questions: how to size, simulate, and operate PV, wind, ESS, backup generation, and controllable
loads when reliability and economics must be evaluated together.
</p>
<ul class="small mb-2">
    <li>Hybrid microgrid design using techno-economic and electrical simulation workflows.</li>
    <li>DER integration studies for data centers, local communities, and remote facilities.</li>
    <li>Programmable control concepts for renewable-rich distribution systems.</li>
</ul>
<p class="small mb-0">
Representative work:
<a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.egyr.2023.02.044" target="_blank">stand-alone hybrid power systems for a data center</a>
and microgrid design studies using HOMER, DIgSILENT, and related tools.
</p>
