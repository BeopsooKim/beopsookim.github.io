<h3 class="h5">Virtual Power Plants and Energy Storage</h3>
<p>
I investigate virtual power plants and energy storage as flexible resources that can coordinate
prosumers, renewable generation, and grid-support services in uncertain operating conditions.
</p>
<p class="small text-muted mb-2">
Core questions: how ESS dispatch, aggregation, and robust real-time decisions affect reliability,
stability, and economic operation from the prosumer level to the feeder level.
</p>
<ul class="small mb-2">
    <li>Prosumer-centric ESS operation strategies and their impact on power-grid reliability.</li>
    <li>Robust VPP operation under renewable and demand forecast uncertainty.</li>
    <li>Decision-search approaches, including Monte Carlo tree search, for real-time operation.</li>
</ul>
<p class="small mb-0">
Representative work:
<a href="https://doi.org/10.5370/KIEE.2021.70.12.1820" target="_blank">ESS operation strategy and grid reliability</a>
and ongoing VPP/ESS control studies.
</p>
