Engineering Implementation

Alongside analytical modeling, I implement power-system methods in software and programmable control hardware. This work connects mathematical models to measurement, computation, and control.

  • GPU-accelerated power-flow calculations and data-driven fault detection.
  • A PLC-based renewable microgrid control panel and control logic for Antarctic observation equipment.
  • A data-center distribution-system testbed for three-phase unbalanced harmonic analysis.
